About us


The University of St. La Salle (USLS) in Bacolod City is a leading Catholic educational institution in Negros Occidental

Campus & Structure

  • Main Campus: Located on La Salle Avenue, Bacolod City, featuring a 10-hectare urban campus.
  • Integrated School (USLS-IS): Operates across campuses on La Salle Ave., Montelibano Dr., and Alunan.
  • Liceo de La Salle: The Senior High School unit, which utilizes a specialized clustering curriculum.

Academic Programs

  1. Yu An Log College of Business and Accountancy
  2. College of Engineering
  3. College of Computing Studies
  4. College of Arts and Sciences
  5. College of Nursing
  6. College of Tourism and Hospitality
  7. College of Education
