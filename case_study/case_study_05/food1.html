Matcha Strawberry

======================================================

======================================================

"Nutrition Facts"

"Matcha is packed with catechins (like EGCG), which help fight

cellular damage and inflammation. It contains L-theanine, an amino acid

that slows the absorption of caffeine, providing sustained focus without

the jitters or crash associated with coffee. Fresh strawberries

provide a natural source of Vitamin C and dietary fiber."

  • Calories: 120 - 250 kcal
  • Total Fat: 3.5g - 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g - 40g
  • Total Sugars: 15g - 28g
  • Protein: 2g - 12g
  • Caffeine: ~ 60 - 70mg

======================================================

"Ingredients"

  • Ceremonial Matcha Powder
  • Strawberries
  • OatMilk
  • Honey
  • White Granulated Sugar
  • Ice Cubes
  • Hot Water

======================================================

"Allergens"

"Safety Warnings"
