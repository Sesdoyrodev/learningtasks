"Burger"

======================================================

"Ingredients"

"the Ingredient listed is for a single burger"

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • 1 Lettuce
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Cheese slices
  • Burger Buns

======================================================

"Steps"

  1. Step 1: Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Step 2: Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  3. Step 3: Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.
  4. Step 4: Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.
  5. Step 5: Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Step 6: Asseble in this order "First bun, then patty, followed by ketchup, mustard, pickles, extra onions, lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, and bun"
  7. Step 7: Serve hot and enjoy!
